Kochi Metro

1. Kochi Metro Rail Project: The Kochi Metro Rail is a rapid transit system serving the city of Kochi. The estimated cost for Phase 1 of the project, which covers a distance of 25.6 kilometers, is approximately ₹5,181 crore ($700 million).

Vizhinjam International Deepwater Multipurpose Seaport

2. Vizhinjam International Deepwater Multipurpose Seaport: The Vizhinjam International Seaport is being developed near Thiruvananthapuram. It is designed to handle large container ships and boost the maritime trade in the region. The estimated cost for the seaport project is around ₹7,525 crore ($1 billion).

KINFRA Integrated Textile Park

3. KINFRA Integrated Textile Park, Palakkad: The Kerala Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation (KINFRA) has developed an Integrated Textile Park in Palakkad district. The park provides infrastructure and support facilities for textile manufacturing units. The estimated investment for this project is around ₹300 crore ($40 million).

Kochi Smart City

4. Kochi Smart City: The Kochi Smart City project is a joint venture between the Government of Kerala and Dubai Holding. It aims to develop an IT and business hub in Kochi. The project is estimated to cost around ₹2,000 crore ($270 million).

Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) Project

5. Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) Project: The DFC project aims to develop high-capacity freight corridors for efficient movement of goods. The total estimated cost for the Eastern and Western DFCs is around ₹81,459 crore ($11 billion).

Bharatmala Pariyojana

6. Bharatmala Pariyojana: It is a road development project aimed at improving connectivity and efficiency of the National Highway network. The estimated investment for the Bharatmala project is around ₹6,92,324 crore ($93 billion).
